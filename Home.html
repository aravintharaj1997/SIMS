Remember that why we work

The system is at the center focus having all the modules such as student, faculty, marks and administration. The student can access the information such as its details, marks. The faculty can view the student details and also the faculty has the right to update the details of student. The faculty can also update the Marks module.

The design and implementation of a comprehensive student information system and user interface is to replace the current paper records. College staff are able to directly access all aspects of a student's academic progress through a sercure, online interface embedded in the college website. All data is thorughtly reviewed and validated on the server before actual record alteration occurs. The system provides a simple interface for the maintenance of student information. Achieving this objective is difficult using a manual system as the information is scattered, can be redundant and collecting relevant information may be very time consuming
